我的旅程

For most people, life is not inherently meaningful. So while we are alive, what matters is the experience of the journey itself, step out into the world, immerse yourself in different places and cultures, and make as many earnest attempts as you can.

对绝大多数人来说，人生没有什么意义。人生在世，重要的是好好体验这一过程：走出去看看这个精彩的世界，体验不同国家不同民族缤纷多彩的文化，尽可能地勇敢地去做更多的尝试。

Here is my stories. :)

  • Burgos Cathedral, Sunlit and Framed in Stained Glass

    装裱在悠久彩色玻璃中的布尔戈斯大教堂

    Burgos, Spain - April 13 2024

    西班牙·布尔戈斯 - 2024年4月13日

    This photo was taken inside the Burgos Cathedral. Through the stained-glass tracery, it captures the main nave bathed in sunlight - as if time itself has paused here, and history has never moved on.

    Burgos is the second major city on the Camino de Santiago's French Route, and the Burgos Cathedral rising from the old town is truly breathtaking. I took a rest day in Burgos, got up early to see off friends I'd met along the way from all over the world, and the morning dew and crisp air felt wonderfully refreshing. From a lookout platform, I admired the exquisitely crafted cathedral from afar - bathed in holy light, it felt like stepping into another world.

    这张照片拍摄于布尔戈斯大教堂内部，隔着彩色玻璃窗花定格沐浴在日光下的大教堂主楼，似乎历史的光阴都在此停留，不曾流逝。

    布尔戈斯是朝圣之路法国线上的第二座大城市，有一座布尔戈斯大教堂坐落在古老的城区，蔚为壮观！我在布尔戈斯休息了一天，早起送别路上遇见的世界各地的朋友们，清晨的露水和空气让人心旷神怡，在平台上远远欣赏着巧夺天工的大教堂，圣光洒下，恍然隔世！

    Paris, France - March 28 2024

    法国·巴黎 - 2024年3月28日

    In my view, Paris has always felt effortlessly laid-back, and Montmartre captures that vibe perfectly. Tucked away from the city's most famous hotspots, Sacré-Cœur stands here on the hill.
